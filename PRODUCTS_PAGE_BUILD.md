### 1HMS - Hospital Management System
**Features:**
- Patient Management (EHR, portal, history, documents)
- Appointments & Scheduling (booking, queue, schedules, reminders)
- Billing & Insurance (invoices, claims, payments, reports)
- Clinical Workflows (e-prescriptions, lab orders, radiology, treatment plans)

**Benefits:**
- Reduce administrative overhead by 40%
- Improve patient satisfaction scores
- Streamline billing and reduce errors
- Complete audit trail for compliance

### 1Rad - Radiology Platform
**Features:**
- DICOM Viewer (multi-modality, 3D, measurements, annotations)
- AI-Assisted Diagnosis (anomaly detection, pattern recognition, comparison, confidence)
- Report Generation (templates, voice dictation, auto-population, signatures)
- Workflow Management (routing, priority, assignment, QA)

**Benefits:**
- Reduce reporting time by 50%
- Improve diagnostic accuracy with AI
- Faster turnaround for critical cases
- Seamless integration with PACS

### 1Lab - Lab Management
**Features:**
- Sample Management (barcode tracking, chain of custody, storage, expiry alerts)
- Test Workflows (catalog, protocols, equipment integration, validation)
- Quality Control (QC protocols, calibration, proficiency testing, audit logs)
- Result Delivery (auto-reporting, critical alerts, patient portal, notifications)

**Benefits:**
- Eliminate sample mix-ups
- Reduce TAT by 30%
- Ensure quality compliance
- Real-time result tracking

### 1Pharma - Pharmacy System
**Features:**
- Inventory Management (stock tracking, expiry, reorder automation, batch tracking)
- Prescription Processing (e-prescription integration, drug interaction checks, dosage validation, substitutions)
- Supply Chain (vendor management, purchase orders, receiving & QC, returns)
- Billing & Insurance (verification, co-pay calculation, claims submission, reconciliation)

**Benefits:**
- Reduce stock-outs by 80%
- Minimize expired inventory
- Improve prescription accuracy
- Optimize working capital
