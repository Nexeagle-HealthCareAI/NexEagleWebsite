## 🎯 Engagement Models

### 1. Project-Based
- Fixed scope, timeline, and budget
- Best for: Well-defined projects with clear requirements
- Duration: 2-6 months typically

### 2. Dedicated Team
- Extended team, monthly retainer
- Best for: Ongoing product development
- Flexibility: Scale team up or down

### 3. Staff Augmentation
- Fill specific skill gaps
- Best for: Short-term needs, specific expertise
- Integration: Work with your existing team
